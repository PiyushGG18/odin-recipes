Margherita Pizza

Margherita Pizza is a classic Italian pizza made with a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella chees, basil leaves, and olive oil. It is known for its simple ingredients and fresh flavor.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220-250C (425-480F)
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a thin circle
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with basil leaves.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and serve hot.
